Care4Elders
Welcome to Care4Elders, your smart companion in managing daily medications and doctor appointments. This application is thoughtfully designed for elderly patients and caregivers to make health management simple, intuitive, and secure.

With Care4Elders, patients no longer need to worry about forgetting their pills or missing a doctor’s appointment. Our integrated digital pill dispenser works seamlessly with the app to remind users when to take each medication. If a dose is missed, the system sends a gentle alert and keeps track of medication history for both patients and doctors.

Beyond medication management, Care4Elders also makes it easy to schedule and manage doctor visits. Whether you're a caregiver, a medical professional, or managing your own treatment plan — Care4Elders is here to support you every step of the way.
